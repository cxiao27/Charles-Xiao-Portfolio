FPV Racing Drone

This is a 5" FPV drone I built the summer after I graduated high school (2024). It features 1900 KV motors, a 6S LiPo Battery, a carbon fiber frame, and analog video footage. Over about the course of a month, I researched and selected components, soldered and assembled the electronics, and configured the flight controller with Betaflight.
